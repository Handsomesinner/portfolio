About Us

We Make China Importation Simple & Safe

PAJS Empire was founded with a single mission: to eliminate the stress, scams, and confusion that Nigerians face when trying to import goods from China. Based in Lagos, we are your trusted partner in navigating the Chinese market — from Alibaba to 1688, Taobao, and beyond.

Over the years, we have helped hundreds of entrepreneurs, resellers, boutique owners, and everyday Nigerians access high-quality products at factory prices. We handle everything: finding the supplier, negotiating prices, placing orders, quality checking, shipping, and door delivery.

With PAJS Empire, you don't need to know Chinese or have any prior import experience. Just tell us what you need — we handle the rest with transparency and care.

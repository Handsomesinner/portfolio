GoSplitly

GoSplitly (often referred to simply as Splitly) is a Nigerian-based platform that allows users to share the costs of premium digital subscriptions. It focuses on making global services more affordable for local users by facilitating "group buys" or cost-sharing for tools like Netflix, TradingView, Canva, and Zoom.

Key Features

  • Cost Sharing: Users pay only a portion of a premium subscription by sharing the account with others.
  • Local Payment Options: Supports Nigerian bank transfers and Paystack, solving the common issue of local cards being declined on international sites.
  • Crypto Payments: Also accepts payments via USDT for those who prefer using cryptocurrency.
  • Marketplace Model: You can either join an existing group for a lower price or create your own to share with others.
